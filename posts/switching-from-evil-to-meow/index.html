Switching From Evil to Meow

Recently I switched from evil model to meow.

Evil mode is powerful but slow. It conflits with the keybindings of some customized modes, like howm or zk’s index mode.

Second, it is difficult to customize for my poor knowledge of elisp.

Last, I only use emacs for note taking, and I don’t need the power of evil mode. I don’t do complex editing, so meow is a good choice.

After read meow-tutor several times and do some customization of key mappings, I am quite happy with meow.
